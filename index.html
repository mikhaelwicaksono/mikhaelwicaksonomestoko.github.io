About Me

I am a 5th semester student from Bina Nusantara University majoring in Computer Science. I live in South Tangerang. my date of birth is Jakarta, 19 February 2001. I am a person who always wants to learn and if I get pressure, I will endure to face it. I can also work as a team. I'm not very fluent in English, but I understand what is meant when someone speaks English. I will keep trying to keep learning and give my best at work.

I can operate HTML, CSS, Java, SQL, Microsoft Office (Word , Power Point and Excel), and Adobe (Illustrator and Photoshop).

My experience is that I once wrote a case study for Tech For Indonesia (TFI) "Dampak Kebersihan Lingkungan pada Proses Belajar Mahasiswa", I was an activist for the Creative and Design Division of HIMTI (Himpunan Teknik Informatika) at Bina Nusantara University. I have participated in ICT International Symposium Participant (BINUS - Polytechnic University of the Philippines).

My quote is that I got from Kerry Katona, which means "Broken wings mean you have to find another way to fly." Which I think if I fail means, I have to find another way to succeed.
